Ben 10 Classic

2007

( season 3 )

This season, Ben travels through a portal to the future and meets his adult self: a round-the-clock hero who can morph into 10,000 alien forms.

Episodes

1. Ben 10,000

23 mins

When Gwen is abducted and taken through a gateway to the future, Ben chases after his cousin -- and gets a look at his adult self.

2. Midnight Madness

23 mins

The Tennysons stop by a mall, where Ben takes part in a hypnotist's show and later starts committing crimes while he's sleepwalking.

3. A Change of Face

23 mins

The Tennysons must again contend with Charmcaster, who intends to swap bodies with Ben to steal the Omnitrix.

4. Merry Christmas

23 mins

While driving through the desert, the Tennysons come upon a strange Christmas village, where Grandpa is mistaken for Santa Claus and taken hostage.

5. Benwolf

23 mins

During a trip to New Mexico, Ben is attacked by a werewolf that turns out to be an evil alien planning to construct a satellite device.

6. Game Over

23 mins

While playing a video game on a rainy day, Ben and Gwen are transported inside the game and must fight their way out.

7. Super Alien Hero Buddy Adventures
23 mins

Ben learns that his alien heroes are starring in a lame network cartoon that has replaced his favorite show, taking his action hero off the air.

8. Under Wraps

23 mins

Believing that his grandchildren need to learn the value of hard work, Max takes them to a farm that puts city slickers to work.

9. The Unnaturals

23 mins

While attending the Little League World Series to watch his home team in action, Ben learns that the opponents are robots masquerading as kids.

10.Monster Weather

23 mins

Max takes the kids to see his favorite golden-oldie band at a music festival in Chicago, where they cross paths with an out-of-control weather robot.

11. The Return

23 mins

At Cape Canaveral, Ben tries to get to the bottom of things when a space shuttle launch is delayed by a purple lightning storm.

12. Be Afraid of the Dark

23 mins

Team Tennyson must try to foil Ghostfreak's plot to plunge Earth into perpetual darkness.

13. The Visitor

23 mins

Ben and Gwen are suspicious when an alien named Xylene, who once had a "relationship" with Max, suddenly appears.

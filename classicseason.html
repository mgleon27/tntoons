Ben 10 Classic

Season 1

2005

13 Episodes

Ten-year-old Ben Tennyson discovers a mysterious device, the Omnitrix, on a family vacation. The device allows him to transform into ten different alien forms replete with unique superpowers.

Season 2

2006

13 Episodes

The plot thickens this season as Grandpa Max reveals his secret past, Ben teams up with a group of alien heroes and Gwen gets superhuman powers.

Season 3

2007

13 Episodes

This season, Ben travels through a portal to the future and meets his adult self: a round-the-clock hero who can morph into 10,000 alien forms.

Season 4

2007

12 Episodes

The summer finally ends. Ben and Gwen have to go back to their normal lives. Grandpa Max tells Ben to keep the Omnitrix a secret from everyone, even his parents. Ben agrees, but Vilgax arrives and he'll stop at nothing to get the Omnitrix.

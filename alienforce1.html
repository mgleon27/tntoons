Ben 10 Alien Force

2008

( season 1 )

Ben Tennyson is a normal teenager who has put away his toys including the Omnitrix that gave him his powers. Ben is attacked by an alien and discovers Grandpa Max is missing. To find his grandpa, Ben decides to put on the Omnitrix.

Episodes

23 mins

Having enjoyed a normal life for several years, Ben dons the Omnitrix once again when Grandpa Max suddenly goes missing.

Ben 10 Classic

2006

( season 2 )

The plot thickens this season as Grandpa Max reveals his secret past, Ben teams up with a group of alien heroes and Gwen gets superhuman powers.

Episodes

1. Truth

23 mins

Max's ex-partner as a plumber, Phil, resurfaces with nefarious intentions. He steals the dangerous Null Void Projector, which puts our heroes in jeopardy.

2. The Big Tick

23 mins

A giant alien tick that 'sucks planets dry' lands in Yellowstone National Park with the sole purpose of spreading its toxins and destroying the earth.

3. Framed

23 mins

Kevin 11 is back, and he wants revenge. Now with the ability to take the shape of several of Ben's alien personalities, Kevin has been committing crimes and framing Ben for them.

4.Gwen 10

22 mins

Gwen gets the Omnitrix and the power that comes with it. Meanwhile a very confused Ben must come to grips with his 'new role' as he teams with Max and 'Gwen 10'.

5. Grudge Match

23 mins

Ben and Kevin 11 are beamed up to a space ship where they're shackled to one another. There, they must learn to fight together.

6. The Galactic Enforcers

23 mins

When an intergalactic trio of alien superheroes arrives in Pittsburgh to track down the bounty hunter Six Six and his alien henchman, Ben joins the trio.

7. The Galactic Enforcers

23 mins

After Max just misses running over a fleeing summer camper on the road, Ben, Max and Gwen take the boy back to his nearly deserted camp.

8. Ultimate Weapon

23 mins

When Max learns that part of an alien weapon has been unearthed in Texas, he becomes obsessed with finding it. In doing so, he leads Ben and Gwen on a wild adventure.

9. Tough Luck

23 mins

Gwen's Lucky Girl powers return when she buys a magical stone at a magic convention in Las Vegas. However Hex is after the same stone.

10. They Lurk Below

23 mins

Max's longtime friend has just built the world's only underwater resort, and Ben, Gwen and Max are invited for a sneak peak. But it turns out that to power the resort.

11. Ghostfreaked Out

23 mins

Ben's internal demons get externalized when Ghostfreak escapes from his watch and becomes a full-fledged being on his own.

12. Dr. Animo and the Mutant Ray

23 mins

Dr. Anino has broken out of jail and, intent on revenge, has been tracking Ben,. At the same time, Ben has broken off a piece of the Omnitrix.

13. Back with a Vengeance

23 mins

Ben's feeling cocky with the watch. He's 'unlocked' its secret, and he can now become any alien he wants, whenever he wants. That's the good news.
